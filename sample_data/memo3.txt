INTERNAL MEMO
To: Executive Team
From: Marcus Johansson, CFO
Date: April 20, 2024
Subject: Q2 Financial Review and Norway/Denmark Expansion

NordMedia Group's Q2 financial performance has exceeded expectations. CFO Marcus 
Johansson confirms that revenue is up 28% compared to Q2 2023, driven primarily 
by the partnership with Oslo Media House and Copenhagen Digital.

The AI platform launched in April by Erik Lindqvist and Anna Bergström has already 
shown measurable impact. DataBridge Solutions has reported a 45% improvement in 
content delivery efficiency since the platform went live.

CEO Sarah Mitchell has approved an additional investment of 5 million SEK into the 
Norway and Denmark expansion. Oslo Media House CEO, Peter Hansen, and Copenhagen 
Digital Director, Maria Andersen, will meet with Sarah Mitchell and Marcus Johansson 
in Stockholm next month to finalize partnership agreements.

TechVentures AB has proposed extending their technical support contract through 2025. 
Lisa Holm from TechVentures AB presented the proposal to Anna Bergström and Erik 
Lindqvist last week.

The Stockholm headquarters renovation managed by Nordic Construction AB is progressing 
on schedule. NordMedia Group expects to return to Stockholm from the Gothenburg 
temporary office by September 2024.

Marcus Johansson projects NordMedia Group will hit annual revenue targets by Q3, 
with further growth expected from the Scandinavia Press partnership finalized by 
Sarah Mitchell in Q1.
